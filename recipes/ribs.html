Ribs

Description

Smoked ribs on the Traeger pellet grill.

Ingredients

  • 2 racks of ribs
  • BBQ sauce
  • BBQ rub
  • Mustard

Steps

  1. Trim ribs if running at an angle.
  2. Pull membranes off.
  3. Apply mustard to each side.
  4. Season generously with rub.
  5. Preheat Traeger to 275.
  6. Place ribs on grill.
  7. Let cook until rub sticks to grill. (~2-2.5 hrs)
  8. Wrap ribs in double foil. Put down more rub, butter, honey, anything you want.
  9. Place wrapped ribs back on grill for 1 hour.
  10. Take ribs off when temperature reaches ~206.
  11. When done, brush on BBQ sauce. Put on wire rack and back on grill for 15-20 mins.
  12. Slice and enjoy.
