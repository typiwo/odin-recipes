Mac n' Cheese

Description

Homemade baked mac n' cheese.

Ingredients

Breadcrumb Topping

  • 58g Panko breadcrumbs
  • 56g melted unsalted butter

Pasta Portion

  • 250g (3c) cheddar cheese
  • 250g (3c) gruyere cheese
  • 1lb macaroni
  • 73g unsalted butter
  • 50g all-purpose flour
  • 720ml whole milk

Steps

  1. Mix breadcrumbs and melted butter, set aside.
  2. Shred cheese, mix together in a bowl, set aside.
  3. Cook macaroni in large pot, boil until just before al dente. Strain, set aside.
  4. In same pot, melt butter over medium heat and add flour. Cook for 45 sec and then slowly whisk in milk until mixture thickens.
  5. Add 4 cups of the cheese mixture, making sure to save the leftover 2 cups. Stir until sauce forms then add back macaroni.
  6. In baking dish, pour in half of mac n cheese, add remaining cheese mixture, then put other half of mac n cheese on top.
  7. Top off with breadcrumb topping.
  8. Place in oven set to 350 for 25 minutes, or until browned and bubbling.
  9. Enjoy.
