Pizza

Description

Homemade cast-iron skillet pizza.

Ingredients

  • 16 oz flour
  • 2 tsp kosher salt
  • 2 1/4 tsp active dry yeast
  • 11 oz room temp water
  • Olive oil
  • 1 28oz can peeled San Marzano tomatoes
  • 2-3 cloves of garlic
  • Dried oregano
  • Dried basil
  • Crushed red pepper
  • Black pepper
  • Low moisture full fat mozzarella block
  • Natural casing pepperoni (cup & char)

Steps

  1. Whisk together flour, salt, and yeast. Add 11oz room temp water and mix until shaggy dough forms w/ no dry clumps (if sticky, add more flour).
  2. Generously oil bowl w/ olive oil. Put in dough and cover w/ plastic wrap. Let rest at room temp for 18-24 hrs.
  3. Preheat oven to max temp for atleast 30 min.
  4. On well-floured surface, divide dough into even pieces.
  5. Pour 2 tbsp olive oil into cast-iron skillet. Drop in dough and gently coax into corners. Cover, let rest 1 hour.
  6. In blender, add can of tomatoes, garlic (rough chopped), shake of oregano, basil, crushed red pepper, salt and pepper. Pulse until smooth.
  7. When dough is done resting, fully stretch to corners. Add sauce, cheese, and toppings.
  8. Place in preheated oven for 12-15 min, or until crispy.
  9. When finished, let rest for 5 min. Slice and enjoy.
